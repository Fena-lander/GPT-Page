5 Things to Know about GPT

As AI technology continues to advance, language models such as GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer) are becoming increasingly powerful tools for a wide range of applications. From chatbots to creative writing, GPT's ability to generate human-like text has the potential to revolutionize the way we interact with technology. However, it's important to understand the limitations of these models as well. In this article, we'll explore five things GPT can do.

One of the key updates made to the model is its ability to handle context and maintain a conversation, making it even more suitable for applications that require a high degree of natural language understanding, such as chatbots and virtual assistants.

The availability of ChatGPT through OpenAI's API and Microsoft's Azure OpenAI Service will make it easier for developers to integrate the model into their own applications and services.
